DEVOTION

  A film by Laura Quirke



------------------------
 
Through candid recordings of dialogue between an Irish mother and her children, ‘Devotion’ raises questions of the sacrifices we make to find joy and purpose in life. These intimate conversations are woven into dreamlike sequences of a young woman living two alternate lives between Dublin city and rural Connemara; pondering the choice of motherhood, the creative pursuit and the ultimate questions of how, why and to whom we choose to devote our time.
